# 📖 Alumni Directory - User Guide

Welcome to the NISER Alumni Archive! This platform is designed to connect students, researchers, and faculty while making it easy to find peers across different graduating batches.


## ✏️ Managing Your Profile

Since this platform serves as an archival directory, strict permissions are enforced. *Only administrators can directly write to or alter the live database.*

If you are a student and want to join the network or update your current details, you use the secure request system:

### 1. Joining the Network
If your details are not on the platform yet, click **Join** in the sidebar. This directs you to an application form.
*   Please fill out all fields carefully.
*   Once submitted, your profile enters the Admin "Action Queue".
*   As soon as an administrator verifies your entry, you will go live on the platform!

### 2. Updating Your Profile
If your profile exists but needs updates (e.g., you changed your job position or want to add a website link):
*   Click **Update** in the sidebar.
*   Type your name exactly as it appears in the system.
*   Fill out the updated fields. **Tip:** You only need to fill out the fields that changed; any blank fields will be ignored by the system, leaving your original data intact!
*   Submit the form. Your request will be flagged as an "Update Request" on the Admin Dashboard for review.

### 3. Direct Messaging the Admin
Need a record deleted? Have a question about the platform? 
*   Click **DM** in the sidebar. 
*   This opens a quick pop-up form. Use this strictly for sending text notes/concerns directly to the developer/admin. Do not use this form to submit your profile details.

## 🔎 Searching & Filtering

The public directory is highly optimized to help you find the exact person you're looking for without needing to click through multiple pages.

### Navigation Sidebar
On the left side (or via the hamburger menu on mobile), you'll see a list of **Directories** organized by Programme and Batch. 
*   **Hierarchy:** Batches are automatically ordered chronologically inside their degree paths (e.g., all *Int. MSc.* batches listed together, sorted newest to oldest, followed by *PhDs*, etc.).
*   Clicking a batch in the sidebar instantly scrolls you to that specific section.

### Advanced Filtering Options
Right at the top of the main window, you will find the floating **Search & Filters** bar.
1.  **Fuzzy Search Box:** Type parts of a name, research interest, or supervisor. It uses "fuzzy matching", meaning if you type `Kamal`, it will correctly find `K. L. Patra`. 
2.  **Dropdown Filters:** You can combine dropdown filters. For example, selecting `Int. MSc.` and `Start Year: 2017` will instantly condense the entire site to only show students matching that exact criteria.
3.  **Reset Button:** Clears all active queries so you can browse the entire dataset again.
